<h2>How to Calculate Your Master Number (60-Second Check)</h2>

<p>Add your full birth date digits. If you hit 11, 22, or 33 before reaching a single digit — stop. That's your master number.</p>

<p><strong>Example:</strong> Born July 29, 1994 → 7 + 2 + 9 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 4 = 41 → 4 + 1 = 5. No master number here.</p>

<p>Born February 9, 1992 → 2 + 9 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 2 = 32 → 3 + 2 = 5. Still no. But born November 9, 1992 → 11 + 9 + 1 + 9 + 9 + 2 = 41... wait. That 11 in November? <strong>Keep it as 11</strong>, don't reduce it to 2. Then: 11 + 9 + 21 = 41 → not a master number life path. However, the 11 still influences through the month position.</p>

<p>Master numbers appear in your Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, or Birthday number. Check all four — most people only check one.</p>

<p><strong>Activation key:</strong> Start recording your first instinct before you rationalize it. Keep a one-line journal — "gut said X" — for 14 days. By day 10, you'll notice the pattern yourself.</p>

<p>Dormant 22 signs: endless preparation, inability to delegate, feeling like your work should be "bigger" than it is — paired with a strange paralysis about scaling up.</p>

<h2>Master Number 33: The Healer's Trap</h2>

<p>33 is the rarest master number in life path calculations. It combines 11's vision with 22's building capacity, directed entirely toward service and healing.</p>

<p>The trap: 33s attract people who need fixing. Endlessly. Without boundaries, the master healer becomes the master martyr.</p>

<p>Dormant 33 signs: resentment disguised as generosity, saying yes when your body screams no, believing your own needs are selfish.</p>

<p><strong>Activation key:</strong> For one week, before agreeing to any request, pause and ask: "Would I recommend this commitment to someone I love?" If the answer is no, that's your answer too. <strong>33 activates through discernment, not sacrifice.</strong></p>
