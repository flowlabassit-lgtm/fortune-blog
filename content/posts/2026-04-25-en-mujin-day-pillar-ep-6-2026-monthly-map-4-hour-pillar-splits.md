<p>戊 is Yang Earth — a mountain, not a field. 辰 is the Water-storage Dragon, which means your day branch quietly holds Water (your wealth element) underneath the Earth. <p>It isn't, quite. The午 branch clashes with no part of 辰 directly, but the year's overall Fire surge dries the hidden Water in your 辰. <strong>Your wealth axis gets thinner exactly when your confidence gets louder.</strong> That mismatch is the whole story of your 2026.</p>

<h2>The contrarian take most Mujin readings miss</h2>

Because Fire feeding Earth makes the mountain bigger, but a bigger mountain needs more water underneath, not less. Watch the cash, not the applause.</p>
