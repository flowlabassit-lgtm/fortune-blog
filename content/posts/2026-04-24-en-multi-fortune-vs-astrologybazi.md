<p>AstrologyBazi (天机玄鉴 / Tianji Xuanjian) positions itself as "AI-empowered Eastern divination." Interpretation comes from a domain-trained AI layer. Boasts "1,000,000+ Readings" as social proof.</p>

<p>Multi Fortune runs a different play. Five systems — Saju (Four Pillars), Tarot, Vedic Astrology, Zi Wei Dou Shu, and Numerology — each produce an independent reading. A convergence layer sits on top, checking every identity dimension (decision style, risk tolerance, communication pattern, relational dynamics, and so on) and flagging where two or more systems reach the same conclusion. The convergence is the signal; a single-system finding is treated as noise until at least one other system agrees.</p>

<h2>What a reading looks like on each</h2>

<h3>AstrologyBazi — depth in one system</h3>

<p>A BaZi reading on AstrologyBazi gives you the full chart — 8 characters, Ten Gods, luck pillars, day pillar archetype — and an AI-generated narrative interpreting each. The commentary is thorough and vertical. If BaZi is your primary lens, the depth rewards the focus.</p>

<h3>Multi Fortune — convergence across five</h3>

<p>A Multi Fortune reading produces a structured identity profile. Each dimension (decision_style, risk_tolerance, communication_pattern, relational_style, career_aptitude, etc.) is scored with the number of systems that independently agree, plus a synthesis sentence and a confidence tier.</p>
